Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam
(1931 - 2015)
Missile Man of India | 11th President of India

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002-2007). Born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he came from a humble background and rose through dedication and hard work.

He played a leading role in India's missile development and nuclear program, earning him the title "Missile Man of India". In 2002, he became the President of India, widely respected for his humility and vision for youth.
